Our Story

Located in Rowland Height, CA, Yei San Jib specializes in traditional Korean dishes. From ancient origins to modern times, Korean cuisine has evolved to reflect each distinct period of Korean history. By bringing together these ancient customary and traditional dishes, our number one goal is to uphold these historical values within our community. We are committed to delivering to the community the best of Korean cuisine at all times, 7 days a week. Everyone is welcome at Yei San Jib!
